Vehicle Shipping Service: Domestic & International

We ship a vehicle and other goods worldwide by air, sea container or Roll On - Roll Of (RORO) transport shipping. RORO ships are large car ferries with no passenger accommodation.

WSP Service is a cost effective method of shipping your car. For cars being shipped with personal effects, shipping by sea container is the most appropriate method. Specialist and high-value cars can be sent by airfreight

We will take care of all the shipping documentation and the necessary export customs formalities. Once your car has departed on the vessel you will be sent the shipping documents and Bill of Lading also advising you of the expected arrival date at the port of importation. On arrival, the vehicle will need to be cleared through customs and you must pay the clearing agent for the local duties, taxes and any other charges from the shipping line related to vehicle handling.

Vehicle Shipping Process

  • SHIPPING IN CONTAINERS

    This is the preferred method for shipping high-value vehicles and classic cars. We can collect your car from any address or you can opt to save some costs and deliver to our nominated depot. Alternatively, we can arrange to deliver the container to your home and the container delivery vehicle will unload the container to ground level to enable the loading of the car. Whichever method you choose our loading team will ‘chock-off’ and securely strap your vehicle into position to ensure safe passage whilst on its journey. Any remaining space – including inside the vehicle – may be utilized your personal belongings/ Household Goods.

  • SHIPPING IN CONSOLIDATION CONTAINERS

    Car Shipping in consolidated containers is more economical than other modes. The car is securely put into the container with two or more cars in it.

  • SHIPPING IN RORO CARRIERS

    The cost-effective way to ship your car to destination ports is shown below. Ro-Ro ships are designed solely to carry cars. Motorbikes, buses, trucks, tanks anything that is on wheels! All vehicles are secured in the ship under cover away from the elements. You may not load the vehicle with any other goods – only the car, spare wheel and any other items that are standard to the car. Why? – because the car and its keys are in the hands of ship operators they are not prepared to take the risk or entertain any claims for any items that may be lost or stolen. If you want to ship goods with your car then we suggest the alternative method of sending your vehicle in a dedicated 20 or 40ft ships container

  • SHIPPING IN CONTAINERS

    We are industry leaders when it comes to the shipment of vehicles by air. Destinations have included GCC, Europe, Canada, Africa and Australia. Cars are often shipped by air to save time and returned to the destination point by sea for cost-saving reasons.

    Air freighting of cars is fast, safe and secure and can be very cost-effective in certain circumstances for highly valuable vehicles.

    Shipping of motorcycle by air (depending on the destination) can often be cost effective particularly if you intend to tour with your bike. If you have a fixed date in mind, it is useful to keep in mind that the ships can and do get delayed and diverted whereas with air cargo services there will always be another flight soon!
